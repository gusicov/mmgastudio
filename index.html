Our standard delivery is under 7 working days from receiving all project files and briefing materials. All deliverables are sent digitally. We always confirm timelines upfront before starting.
We work with CAD drawings, Revit files, SketchUp models, or any 3D format you have available. Reference images for materials, mood boards, and a clear brief are also very helpful. The more detail you provide, the closer the first draft will be to your vision.
All our packages include two rounds of revisions. We begin with a low-resolution preview (grey-box stage) for composition approval before finalizing materials and lighting — this keeps revisions focused and efficient. Additional rounds can be accommodated at an agreed rate.
